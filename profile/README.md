# WinMo

**Multi-chain DeFi portfolio management across Ethereum and Solana.**

WinMo gives you a single interface to connect wallets from both chains, view unified holdings, trade tokenized assets, explore yield farming, and execute forex swaps — all powered by on-chain oracles and DEX aggregators.

---

### Thesis

Traditional finance is fragmented — your stocks sit in one brokerage, crypto in another wallet, forex in a third platform, and yield opportunities require yet another interface. On-chain, it's worse: every chain is its own silo with its own wallets, tokens, and UX.

WinMo exists because we believe **every financial asset will be tokenized, and no one should need five apps to manage them**. Stocks, currencies, commodities, bonds, and crypto are converging on-chain through RWAs — but the infrastructure to use them as a unified portfolio doesn't exist yet.

We're building that layer. One interface where a user connects their Ethereum and Solana wallets, sees their entire net worth across tokenized equities, stablecoins, forex positions, and yield strategies — and can act on any of it in one click. Not a CEX. Not a bridge. A portfolio OS for the tokenized economy.

The opportunity is the gap between TradFi's $600T in global assets moving on-chain and the current DeFi UX that still thinks in terms of single-chain, single-token interactions. WinMo closes that gap.

---

### What we build

- **Unified Portfolio** — Merge ERC-20 and SPL token holdings into one dashboard with real-time pricing
- **Asset Trading** — Trade crypto, tokenized stocks, commodities, bonds, and RWAs via KyberSwap (Ethereum) and Jupiter (Solana)
- **Forex on Solana** — Live FX rates from Pyth Network oracles, cross-rate matrix, stablecoin arbitrage detection, and direct currency swaps
- **Yield Discovery** — Browse and compare yield protocols across chains with risk-tiered filtering
- **AI Suggestions** — Heuristic engine that surfaces arbitrage signals, carry trade opportunities, and portfolio rebalancing actions
